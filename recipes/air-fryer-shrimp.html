Air Fryer Shrimp

Description

This coconut shrimp air fryer recipe comes out great and is delicious served with a simple spicy honey-lime dip.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened flaked coconut
  • 1/3 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 12 oz uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Stir together flour and pepper in a dish. Lightly beat eggs in a second dish. Stir together coconut and panko in a third dish.
  2. Dredge shrimp in flour mixture one at a time, shaking off excess. Place shrimp on plate and coat with cooking spray.
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F and add 1/2 of the shrimp, cooking 3 minutes. Turn shrimp over and continue cooking, about 3 minutes more. Season with 1/4 teaspoon salt and repeat with remaining shrimp.
  4. Whisk together honey, lime juice, and serrano chile in a small bowl and servy with shrimp.
